More data faster, COMPARED to radio waves

In contrast to radio waves, the infrared light used for laser communications packs data into smaller waves, allowing stations to receive more information simultaneously. Thus, more data can be delivered in a single downlink even though laser communications aren't "faster" in terms of velocity.

COMMUNICATIONS BEYOND THE SOLAR SYSTEM

The Gbit network can expand beyond our solar system, so that humans can communicate with each other and can contact Earth even when beyond the orbit of Pluto, the planet farthest away from our Sun.
