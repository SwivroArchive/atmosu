The future of space travel

THE ONLY WAY HUMANITY CAN SURVIVE LONG-TERM IS WITH A SYSTEM LIKE THE ALCUBIERRE WARP DRIVE.

We need Warp Drive Technology

According to our math, you'd have to be traveling 2.6280406e+13 m/s, or, 87,662 times the speed of light, to travel 1 light year in 6 minutes. We believe that we'd need to have the capability to travel at such speeds if we, humanity, would like to have an increased chance at discovering the origins of the universe, finding and observing intelligent alien life, discovering complex "anomalies" in space, and surviving in the long-term.
As far as we know, the only way that this could hypothetically be achieved is via an system like the Alcubierre Warp Drive (which is currently only a concept).
This type of system does not technically break the laws of physics, however, it requires negative energy/mass, which is currently not accessible to us. Perhaps when humanity eventually finds a way to efficiently contain and move around dark energy, we will begin the development/construction of a real warp drive.

Faster than light travel in space

As far as we know, one of the only ways we can travel faster than the speed of light is via a warp drive system, like the Alcubierre Warp Drive. Faster than light travel is essential for humanity in the long-term.

Enabling deep exploration

With Warp Drives, we can travel across and explore the universe at a much faster rate. We must explore the cosmos if we want any chance at finding the origins of everything, discovering intelligent alien life, and more.

HELP US TRANSITION TO A TIME OF WARP DRIVE TECHNOLOGY

Humanity must learn to outgrow its politics, nations, and differences. We need to realize that none of these things really matter at the end of the day. We must work toward advancing our space technologies. It's the only way humanity advances.
